Hello Tarot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2025

"Hello Tarot" (the "App") values your privacy and complies with applicable laws. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: Email, nickname, auth provider (social/email), temporary reset tokens.
  • Usage: Tarot questions/cards/interpretations, shared links, journal memos, feedback, usage limits, error/access logs.
  • Device/Logs: Device/OS, IP region/timezone, app version, crash reports, access logs.
  • Analytics/Ads IDs (if enabled): Advertising/analytics identifiers, cookies-like IDs.
  • Payments: Store receipt/order IDs, purchase status (no card details stored by us).
  • Notifications: Push tokens for service alerts.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • Service: Tarot analysis, saving/viewing history, shared links.
  • Safety/Fraud: Abuse prevention, rate limits, error/abuse investigation.
  • Payments/Support: Purchase confirmation, refunds/CS via app stores.
  • Personalization/Marketing: Recommendations and optional notifications (you can opt out in app/OS settings).
  • Analytics/Performance: Reliability and quality improvement.

3. Processors and International Transfers

We may entrust processing to specialized partners and transfer data internationally to deliver the service. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

  • Google LLC (US): AI tarot interpretation; analytics (if enabled).
  • Supabase Inc. (US): Database storage & account management.
  • Slack Technologies (US): Error monitoring (may include limited identifiers).
  • App stores/payment processors: Billing, receipts, refunds.

4. Retention and Deletion

Data is kept while you use the service. You can delete your account anytime in “Settings > Delete Account”; related personal data is deleted immediately through cascade rules. Data we must keep for law/fraud/dispute (e.g., receipts, abuse logs) is kept separately for the minimum required period, then deleted. Tokens are invalidated on deletion.

5. Your Rights and Contact

You may request access/rectification/erasure/export/restriction, withdraw consent, or opt out of marketing/push in app/OS settings. Contact: support@hellotarot.app.
